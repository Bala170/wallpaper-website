About Looksy

Meet the Team

We are a passionate group of designers, developers, and digital art enthusiasts dedicated to bringing you the best wallpaper experience.

In a world where we spend more time on screens than anywhere else, Looksy was born from a simple question: Why not make every glance beautiful? We believe your screen is your daily window to the world — and it deserves to reflect your mood, your moments, and your mindset. Looksy exists to transform plain pixels into personal art.

We’re not just building a wallpaper site; we’re cultivating a culture. Looksy connects people through visuals — one background at a time. It’s a place where art becomes atmosphere. Whether you're setting the tone for productivity, calm, creativity, or chaos — there’s a visual here for that. And the best part? It's all curated with care, not algorithms.

Beyond downloads, Looksy is a platform for discovery — of art, emotion, and expression. We support emerging artists, showcase hidden gems, and invite the community to not just consume, but contribute. Because at the heart of Looksy is a belief: your screen isn’t just something you use — it’s something that should inspire you every time it lights up
